[  Jesus loves you  ]


Do you feel empty? Do you need love?

I'd like to introduce someone who really loves you, and even died for you.

His name is Jesus.

He is son of God, and our Christ.

Now I will tell you Four Spiritual Laws. It's about the plan for you.


1. God loves you and has a wonderful plan for you.

(John 3:16) For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.


2. Human is away from God in sin. Therefore, he or she cannot know God's love and plan and experience it.

(Isaiah 59:1) Surely the arm of the Lord is not too short to save, nor his ear too dull to hear.


3. Jesus Christ is God's only way to solve human sins. You know God's love and plan for you through him, and you experience it.

(Relevation 21:8) But the cowardly, the unbelieving, the vile, the murderers, the sexually immoral, those who practice magic arts, the idolaters and all liars -- they will be consigned to the fiery lake of burning sulfur. This is the second death.


4. We should personally welcome Jesus Christ as my Savior, my God. Then we know God's love and plan for each person and experience it.

(John 14:6) Jesus answered, "I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me.



God loves you so much and he is still waiting for you.

I will pray for you.
